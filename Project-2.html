SAP Run Live Website

Overview

We built a customized website for SAP to promote its new business platform – Live Business. The primary objective of the SAP Live Business website was to create a responsive experience to attract and assist SAP’s customers to transform from traditional system into a digital real-time business platform. We successfully executed our vision by applying visual data and interactive real-time experience into the website The project was delivered in under six months and received overwhelmingly positive feedback from SAP executives team and we launched the new website at SAP annual conference SAPPHIRE NOW with over 20,000 attendees.
